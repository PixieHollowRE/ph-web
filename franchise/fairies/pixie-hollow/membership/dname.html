You need to set up a D-Name. Your D-Name is a unique name associated with your account to protect your personal identity. When you are signed in, your D-Name also helps your friends find you no matter which Fairy you are playing in Pixie Hollow, as well as in other Disney.com games.

Once a D-Name is chosen it cannot be changed so make sure to choose something really flitterific!

D-Name Info

Your D-Name is what you will be known as throughout the Disney community. It cannot include any personal information, such as your first name, your e-mail address or where you live.

Example of an approved D-Name: Super_Fairy

Example of a denied D-Name: Kristin_Smith.

Once you enter a D-name, it will be submitted for approval and you will be assigned a temporary D-Name. Within 72 hours your D-Name will be approved or denied. If your D-Name is denied, the next time you login you will be prompted to pick a new one.

Once a D-Name is approved it cannot be changed so make sure to choose something really flitterific!
