Concerned about updating your Flash Player? Please Contact Us.
 
Please note: In order to ensure that your Flash Player updates correctly, you should close any programs that might utilize Flash -- such as, instant messaging applications or browsers.

If you continue to have problems, you can try installing the updated Flash Player directly from Adobe or you can read more about how to uninstall your current version of Flash.
